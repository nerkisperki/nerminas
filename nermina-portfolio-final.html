Projects

01

Patient Education · Non-Profit · Personal Project

IVF 360° — A Virtual IVF Exhibition

IVF is often discussed in a highly clinical way, while the emotional reality of people going through fertility treatment is rarely acknowledged. IVF 360° is a personal, non-profit digital project created to make conversations around IVF more open, understandable, and compassionate.

Designed as a virtual exhibition, it offers a safe, neutral space where people can explore IVF at their own pace — without pressure, judgment, or medical jargon. Human-first, not system-first.

02

Digital Health · UX/UI Concept · Merck

Merck Fertility App — Simplifying IVF Medication

Self-administering fertility medications during an IVF cycle is one of the most stressful parts of treatment. Injection schedules, dosage changes, and timing windows leave patients vulnerable to errors — at the worst possible moment.

This app concept was developed to guide patients step-by-step through their stimulation protocol, reducing complexity and minimising mistakes. Designed in Figma with a focus on clarity, calm, and clinical accuracy.
